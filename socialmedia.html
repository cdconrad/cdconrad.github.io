Social Media & Disinformation

Social media plays such an important role in our lives that some people think we are living in a “post digital” world; in other words, it doesn’t make sense to separate our digital from our real-world lives. Whether you agree or disagree with that statement, it is hard to deny that social media disinformation it impacts our society. One of the reasons that it is so impactful is that it is hard for us to distinguish false information in a short amount of time.

We are researching ways to design social media so that it is harder for disinformation to gain traction. We are doing this by assessing the most effective ways to notify people about it.
