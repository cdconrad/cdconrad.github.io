CORG

Cognition and Organizations Research Group

We are a user experience and technology research group based out of Dalhousie University in Halifax, Canada.

This site is a work in progress. Check back soon for updates.

Educational Technology

What happens in your mind when you watch long lectures videos or engage in chat apps? We are investigating the best ways to learn online by observing the brain.

Social Media

Companies built social media to keep users scrolling. We are investigating ways to help people stay attentive and critical of misinformation.

Remote Work

Remote work is hard, but some organizations are really good at it. We are finding ways to transfer those skills to a broader audience and to more people.
