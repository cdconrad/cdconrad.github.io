Mind Wandering & Educational Technology

Mind wandering is the experience of losing sense of your surroundings and directing your attention towards self-generated thoughts. There are many perspectives on what mind wandering is, though most of them agree that it is, at least in part, a function of your brain's default mode network. Mind wandering is probably an important part of our cognitive functioning, though some scholars believe that it can get in the way of learning.

Our work on mind wandering concerns educational technology, with the aim of finding new ways to create educational technologies that better keep our sustained attention.
