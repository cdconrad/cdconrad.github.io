Remote Work & Virtual Offices

During social distancing caused by the Covid-19 pandemic, many office workers adopted remote work. In the days since, it has become clearer that remote work will continue to play an important role in the economy, even if it won't become the main way of doing office work. With the promise of better work-life balance, many organizations are now looking for new ways to integrate remote work into their business models,

We launching a new initiative to investigate ways to make remote work more livable and equitable for everyone.
